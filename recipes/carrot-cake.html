Carrot Cake

Where sweetness meets indulgence

A scrumptious fusion of flavors where the sweetness of carrots meets the indulgence of cake. It's like a surprise packageâ€”moist, dense, and dotted with shreds of carrots that add both texture and a hint of natural sweetness. Topped off with a creamy layer of frosting, it's the perfect blend of cozy and decadent.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups grated carrots
  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/2 cup vegetable oil
  • 3 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 1/2 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 cup chopped nuts (walnuts or pecans)

For frosting:

  • 8 oz cream cheese, softened
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 2 cups powdered sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Combine grated carrots, sugar, oil, and eggs in a bowl, mixing until well blended for a moist base.
  2. In another bowl, mix flour, baking soda, baking powder, cinnamon, and salt for added texture and flavor.
  3. Gradually add the dry mixture to the carrot mix, gently stirring until combined; fold in chopped nuts.
  4. Pour the batter into a prepared pan and bake until a toothpick comes out clean.
  5. Cream together softened cream cheese, butter, powdered sugar, and vanilla extract for frosting.
  6. Once the cake is cool, generously spread the cream cheese frosting.
  7. Slice and enjoy the homemade carrot cake!
