Causa limeña

Sunshine on a plate

Creamy mashed potatoes infused with zesty lime, layered with savory chicken or tuna, and kissed by a hint of spice. A Peruvian delight that's as vibrant as the coastal city it hails from—each bite is a burst of flavor, a fiesta on your taste buds!

Ingredients

  • Boiled yellow potatoes
  • Lime juice
  • Yellow chili pepper paste (aji amarillo)
  • Oil (usually vegetable oil)
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Avocado
  • Chicken or tuna (for the filling)
  • Mayonnaise

Steps

  1. Boil and peel the yellow potatoes. Mash them thoroughly in a bowl.
  2. Add lime juice, aji amarillo paste, a drizzle of oil, salt, and pepper to the mashed potatoes. Mix until well combined.
  3. Prepare the filling by cooking and shredding chicken or mixing tuna with mayonnaise.
  4. Create layers in a square mold: start with a potato mixture layer, then add the filling, and repeat, ending with a final layer of potato.
  5. Chill the assembled dish in the refrigerator for at least an hour to set.
  6. Garnish the chilled causa with avocado slices and serve chilled, cut into squares. Enjoy your Causa Limeña!
