Ceviche

Freshness in every bite

Experience the ocean's delight with this exquisite dish—freshly caught bonito marinated in citrus juices, tossed with vibrant vegetables, and seasoned to perfection. It's a burst of coastal flavors in every refreshing mouthful!

Ingredients

  • Bonito fillet, fresh and cleaned
  • Limes or lemons, freshly squeezed
  • Red onion, thinly sliced
  • Tomato, diced
  • Cilantro, finely chopped
  • Jalapeño or other chili, diced (optional for heat)
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Avocado, sliced for garnish
  • Tortilla chips or tostadas, for serving

Steps

  1. Cut the bonito fillet into bite-sized pieces and place in a bowl.
  2. Squeeze fresh lime or lemon juice over the bonito, ensuring it's fully covered. Let it marinate for 15-20 minutes.
  3. Add sliced red onion, diced tomato, finely chopped cilantro, and diced chili (if using) to the marinated bonito. Mix gently.
  4. Season with salt and black pepper to taste. Stir well to combine all the flavors.
  5. Chill the ceviche in the refrigerator for about 10-15 minutes to let the flavors meld.
  6. Garnish with sliced avocado before serving. Serve with tortilla chips or tostadas and enjoy the freshness of your Ceviche of Bonito!
